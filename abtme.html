About Me

All my life I have been passionate about creativity and have had a passion for creating visual art from a young age. Everything from drawing, painting, origami, you name it and I most likely had a period where I was "obsessed" with it. Therefore, I always knew that my future education had to allow me to be creative, and use the skills I have built up from a young age. I therefore chose to study graphic design, so that I can use my skills to help others, through good design strategies.
Whether it's creating a personal brand,posters business cards, catalogs etc.
I am now studying at Noroff Oslo, and aim to create a solid portfolio, as well as to build a good network of contacts, in order to maximize the chances of landing a dream job upon completion of education. I am eager to learn and develop my skills as much as possible, as well as expand my network
